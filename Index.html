Games Rules & Regulations

Please read the rules carefully before participating. The aim is to keep every game safe, fair, and enjoyable for all age groups.

```

General Instructions

1. Registration Participants must register before the game starts and follow the instructions of the coordinators.
2. Fair play Any form of cheating, unsafe conduct, or misbehavior may lead to disqualification.
3. Safety first Children should be accompanied by parents or guardians where required.
4. Time management Be present at the venue at least 10 minutes before your event.

Game Categories

  • Kids games
  • Family games
  • Ladies games
  • Gents games
  • Team games

Scoring & Results

  • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • Scores may be based on time, accuracy, or points.
  • In case of a tie, the organizer may conduct a tie-breaker.

Important Notes

  1. Use the game area only for the assigned activity.
  2. Follow lane markings, queue lines, and marshals’ directions.
  3. Return borrowed items or props after the game.
  4. Respect other participants and maintain sportsmanship.
  5. Organizers reserve the right to modify rules if required.
For any clarification, please contact the event volunteers or the communication team at the venue.
```
